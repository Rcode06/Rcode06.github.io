About Me


I am originally from Mexico City, love mexican food and I am so happy I know how to cook it. I moved in USA since 1993 and I have had the opportunity to live in different states, in Utah where I have seen the most beautiful mountain call Timpanogos, I lived there for 9 years and I really loved it, I lived in San Diego, it has an awesome weather and many places to visit, then I lived in Wisconsin, the coldest state in USA that is what I think, in winter as soon as you put you foot out of the door your nose turn red and your ears are so cold and hard if someone touch them, they could break, but I really like its landscapes and the cheese. Now I live in AZ a happy state because the mayority of the days the sun is out, different landscapes I really like living here.
